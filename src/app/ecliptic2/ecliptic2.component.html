ecliptic 2

The sequel to the hit orbital mechanics game is here!

Complete six more levels filled with diverse challenges!

Complete every mission and go for the high score!

ecliptic 2 is the sequel to ecliptic, my 2D orbital mechanics game. Unlike the original, ecliptic 2 features planets you can land on and multiple mission types.

Use the left and right arrows to rotate your ship, and use the up arrow to thrust, but watch your fuel! Press 'Z' to shoot asteroids.

You can zoom out by pressing 'shift', or see the entire level by pressing 'esc'.

You can press 'R' to retry from the checkpoint at any time, though this will count as crashing your ship.

ecliptic 2 also supports analogue control with gamepads, although results may be vary.
